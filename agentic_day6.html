📈 Evaluation Metrics

What & Why Measure?

Success Rate: % of queries resolved correctly. Steps taken: reasoning complexity. Token cost: operational expense. Tool accuracy: correct tool selection rate.

Without observability, agents are black boxes. Metrics enable benchmarking, regression detection, and cost optimization.
